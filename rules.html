This page is for rules that users must abide against in the Twitch chat of peregrineotter



Definitions

The term “chat” refers to the online service provided by Twitch that enables users to communicate using text-based messages.

The term “person” and “persons” refers to a user of Twitch.

The term “moderator” refers to a user with elevated permissions over the regular user.

The term “timeout” refers to the action taken against a user that prevents the ability to send text-based messages for a specified amount of time.


Code of Conduct

Hateful Comments

Harassment, verbal abuse, racism, sexism, and targeting will not be tolerated.

Punishment Minimum: 10-minute timeout unless racism or sexism, then it is a ban.


NSFW Language

No sexualized comments towards Peregrineotter or anyone in the chat.

Punishment Minimum: 1-minute timeout


Spam

Spam of any kind will not be tolerated.

Punishment Minimum: 1-minute timeout, continued spam 10 minutes.


Toxicity

No toxicity is tolerated

Punishment Minimum: 10-minute timeout


Asking/Begging

Do not ask for VIP, moderator, gifted subscriptions, or to play with Peregrineotter

Punishment Minimum: Warning


Moderation action

Do not discuss a moderation action in a public chat

Punishment Minimum: Warning


English

Keep chat english so mods can understand what is being said.

Punishment Minimum: Warning


Self promotion

No self promotion is allowed in twitch chat.

Punishment Minimum: Warning
